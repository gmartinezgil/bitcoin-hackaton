📖 How to Read This Chart

The Monthly Savings Race: This chart simulates investing a fixed amount (like $1,000 MXN) every single month, starting from your chosen date. It shows your total accumulated wealth over time.

  • 🟢 CETES & 🔵 AFORE: The Anchor. These grow slowly and predictably through compound interest. This is your foundation for retirement and emergencies.
  • 🟠 Bitcoin: The Rocket. It experiences extreme highs and lows. While it carries more risk, buying a little bit every month (DCA) smooths out the crashes and captures the massive growth peaks.
  • 🟣 USD/MXN (Dashed Line): The Shield. Bitcoin is priced in dollars. If the Mexican Peso loses value (the dashed line goes up), your Bitcoin actually becomes worth more in Pesos, protecting your purchasing power!
💡 Street Advice for your Golden Years: Don't pick just one! Let your employer build your AFORE, use CETES for cash you might need next year, and DCA a small amount into Bitcoin every month to protect against inflation and currency devaluation over the next decade.
